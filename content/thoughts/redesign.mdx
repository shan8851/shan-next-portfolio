My personal website has seen many many iterations over the past few years. I like to keep changing things up and it is often the place I like to play around with new things for the first time.


I feel like the last version of my site was the most dull yet. I tend to go through phases. For whatever reason the last time I just wanted to focus on content and not worry too much about design or any of the fluff. I still feel like that for the most part. But, I wanted a small change. I wanted to capture a little more of my personality without going ott.

I spend a lot of time in the terminal these days and have for some time, so I thought why not emulate a small terminal with the personal site. Don't get me wrong, I know it is not the easiest to figure out and navigate. I'm not sure that really matters though. Let's be honest, nobody really comes here and if they do they are in the software game and should be able to figure things out 😄.

Quick post, just introducing the new site. I have a fairly big piece of news I will share in the upcoming weeks. Watch this space!
